Ghost

Ghost is a walkthrough attraction which combines engineering, creative arts, automation, animation and software capability to deliver the most advanced and near realistic attraction in this segment. Ghost attraction delivers multiple surprising events, encompasses fear and makes one confused on the possibility of occurrences of the activities through their naked eye. It comprise of various static props, animatronics props, digital (animated) props, complete theming, required audio systems for ambient and activity.

Fantasy Zone

Fantasy Zone is the Next Generation Interactive Shooting Game suitable for age group from 5 – 6 Years to All Ages! This will be a highly addictive and Interactive Gaming which will attract all age groups. In Fantasy Zone the gamers will be provided with the Hover Boards to move around the Fantasy Zone. The gamers shall also be provided with Guns with which they will be able to shoot the various targets inside the Fantasy Zone.
